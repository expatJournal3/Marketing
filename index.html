Where does your path lead?

When we travel, we create beautiful memories. Memories that we never forget. But why stop there? With Expath, sharing your memories with the world is simple and fun. Keep track of all your trips with photos and journals. Follow your friends journeys and never miss a moment. You can even comment on your friends journals and let them know what you think. Lets keep track of your adventures together!

What is Expath?

Expath is an all in one blog solution designed specifically for expatriates and travelers. We help you log your adventures and follow others on their travels. Whether you're sharing a mountain climb with the world or logging a private trip that only close friends and family can access, Expath is right there with you.

Take Us With You

Use Expath through our mobile application or on your browser. Either way, it's easy. Whether you're on a mountain or floating down a river, you can access Expath anywhere.

Find Yourself

Reflect on your journeys as you fill your travel blogs. Learn more about yourself every day. Show the world who you really are.
